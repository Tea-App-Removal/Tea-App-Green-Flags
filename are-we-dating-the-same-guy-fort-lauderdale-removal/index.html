Florida Defamation Laws and Your Rights

Florida provides strong legal protections against defamation under Florida Statute § 770.01-770.02. Defamation in Florida encompasses false statements of fact published to third parties that cause reputational harm. Florida courts have consistently applied these protections to online and social media content.

Florida recognizes defamation per se for statements accusing someone of criminal behavior, professional incompetence, or a loathsome disease.
