Arizona Defamation Laws and Your Rights

Arizona provides strong defamation protections under Arizona Revised Statutes § 12-651 through § 12-654 and Arizona common law. Arizona law recognizes both libel and slander and allows recovery of actual and punitive damages for false statements damaging reputation.

Arizona recognizes defamation per se for false statements imputing criminal conduct, a loathsome disease, professional incompetence, or unchastity. AWDTSG posts in Tucson frequently contain accusations falling within these categories, meaning damages are presumed under Arizona law.
