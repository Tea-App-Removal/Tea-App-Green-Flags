Alabama Defamation Laws and Your Rights

Alabama provides strong defamation protections under Alabama Code § 6-5-180 through § 6-5-189. Alabama law recognizes both libel and slander and provides for recovery of general and punitive damages for false statements that damage reputation.

Alabama recognizes defamation per se for false statements imputing criminal behavior, a loathsome disease, professional unfitness, or unchastity. Many Birmingham AWDTSG posts contain accusations within these categories, meaning damages are legally presumed without requiring proof of specific financial losses.
