    Colorado Defamation Laws and Your Rights

    Colorado provides clear legal protections against defamation under C.R.S. § 13-21-102 and common law principles.

    Key aspects of Colorado defamation law for Denver residents:

  • Defamation per se in Colorado covers false statements imputing criminal conduct, a loathsome disease, sexual misconduct, or harm to business, trade, or profession — no special damages required
  • Colorado's statute of limitations for defamation is one year from publication
  • Colorado's anti-SLAPP provisions exist but are narrower than some states and do not protect provably false statements of fact
  • Actual and presumed damages are available in per se cases
  • Colorado courts have recognized online publication as meeting defamation requirements
