    California Defamation Laws and Your Rights

    California provides robust legal protections against defamation. The same California Civil Code protections that cover Los Angeles apply to San Francisco residents.

    Key aspects of California defamation law for San Francisco residents:

  • Libel per se (Cal. Civ. Code § 45) applies when false statements impute criminal conduct, sexual misconduct, or professional incompetence — no special damages required
  • California's statute of limitations is one year from publication
  • General damages are presumed in libel per se cases, covering emotional distress and reputational harm
  • California Civil Code § 1708.85 provides additional protections against online harassment
  • California's anti-SLAPP statute (CCP § 425.16) exists but does not protect provably false statements of fact
