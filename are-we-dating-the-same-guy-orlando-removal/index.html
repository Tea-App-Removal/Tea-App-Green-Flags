    Florida Defamation Laws and Your Rights

    Florida's defamation laws protect Orlando residents facing false AWDTSG posts. Under Florida Statute § 770.01, defamation encompasses false statements of fact published to third parties that damage reputation.

    Florida recognizes defamation per se for statements accusing someone of criminal conduct, imputing a loathsome disease, or alleging professional unfitness. False Orlando AWDTSG posts containing accusations of abuse, criminal behavior, or sexual misconduct typically qualify as defamation per se, meaning damages are presumed under Florida law.
