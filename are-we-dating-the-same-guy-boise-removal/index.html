Idaho Defamation Laws and Your Rights

Idaho provides defamation protections under Idaho Code § 6-702 through § 6-712. Idaho law recognizes defamation as false and unprivileged publications that damage reputation, and Idaho courts have applied these principles to online and social media content.

Idaho recognizes defamation per se for false statements imputing criminal behavior, a loathsome disease, professional unfitness, or unchastity. AWDTSG posts in Boise's group frequently contain accusations falling within these categories, meaning damages are presumed without proof of specific financial harm.
